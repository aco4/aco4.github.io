EFFECTIVE BUTTON DESIGN 101

Visual

- Shape is usually rectangular, optionally rounded. Circles work too, but no weird shapes.

- Must look like a touchable, clickable object: For example, by adding button shadow.

- The button's colour should stand out from the rest of the webpage to grab attention

Interactive feedback

- Button and mouse should visually change when hovering over it

- Button should visually change when pressed

Text

- Use an explicit call to action. ("Sign up now" instead of "Click here for signup")

- Use first person. ("See my result" instead of "See your result")
